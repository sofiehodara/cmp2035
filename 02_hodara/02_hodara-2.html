about

The Future of Information Communication | Chapter 3, 2015: Visualized returns to New York to bring together the world's most creative minds that are changing the way we communicate, understand, and interact with data. Join hundreds of data designers, visualizers, storytellers and technologists for an experiential three days, October 7, 8 & 9, as we explore the future of information communication.

speakers

The main stage will feature 35+ speakers that will present 45-minute-data-driven visual narratives. Designed to be as educational as they are entertaining and inspirational, presentations will span across a number of disciplines and subject matters, but all keeping with the theme of exploring new ways to communicate information.

attend

who should attend?

Professionals and creative thought leaders working in data visualization / data science / UX design / graphic design / media publishing / business intelligence / data-driven visual storytelling of any kind

Visualized is a smart, progressive and fun event. We pack it full of surprises and goodies for attendees. It feels more like an energetic community than a stuffy conterence, so dress any way you want and please come in with an open mind and be respectful to others. If you do, there is a good chance you’ll walk away with new friends, potential future collaborators and inspiration to create data experiences, and share your data, in ways you haven’t considered before.
